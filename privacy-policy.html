Privacy Policy for DiskCleanMaster

Effective Date: 2026-05-21

DiskCleanMaster ("the App") is a macOS utility developed by liangxiaozhan ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how DiskCleanMaster accesses, processes, and stores data when you use the App.

1. Overview

DiskCleanMaster is designed as a local-first disk analysis and cleanup tool for macOS.

By default, the App analyzes files and folders locally on your Mac. It is intended to help you review storage usage, identify large files, and clean up files or folders you choose to remove.

2. Information the App Accesses

DiskCleanMaster may access the following information on your device:

  • Folder and file paths that you explicitly authorize the App to scan
  • File and folder metadata, such as name, size, type, package status, hidden status, and other filesystem attributes needed to analyze storage usage
  • Local permission state, including whether Full Disk Access has been granted
  • App preferences, such as scan settings and file size thresholds

The App only scans folders that you explicitly select or authorize.

3. Full Disk Access

DiskCleanMaster may offer an option to open macOS Privacy & Security settings so you can grant Full Disk Access.

Full Disk Access is optional. It is used only to improve scan coverage for protected locations on your Mac, such as Mail, Messages, and certain Library content.

Granting Full Disk Access does not change the App's core privacy model: file analysis is still intended to occur locally on your device.

4. How Your Data Is Processed

DiskCleanMaster is designed to process scan results locally on your Mac.

The App uses the information described above to:

  • Analyze disk usage
  • Categorize files and folders
  • Display storage summaries and treemap visualizations
  • Identify files or folders above a selected size threshold
  • Perform cleanup actions you explicitly trigger, such as Move to Trash or Delete Immediately

DiskCleanMaster is not designed to upload your file contents to remote servers as part of its normal end-user functionality.

5. Local Storage

To improve usability, DiskCleanMaster stores certain information locally on your Mac, including:

  • Security-scoped bookmarks or equivalent local authorization records for folders you approved
  • App preferences and scan settings
  • Interface state needed to restore your experience

This local data is stored on your device and is used to remember the folders and settings you have chosen.

6. Cleanup Actions

DiskCleanMaster may allow you to take cleanup actions on files or folders you select.

These actions may include:

  • Move to Trash, which uses the macOS Trash and may allow later recovery
  • Delete Immediately, which permanently removes items and may not be recoverable

You are responsible for reviewing the selected items before confirming cleanup actions.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not use the App to share your file contents with third parties as part of normal app functionality.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs in the App's core functionality, unless otherwise disclosed in a future update to this Privacy Policy.

8. Third-Party Services

If a future version of DiskCleanMaster introduces third-party services, remote diagnostics, analytics, or cloud-based features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Children's Privacy

DiskCleanMaster is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the App's behavior aligned with a local-first privacy model. However, no software can guarantee absolute security, and you are responsible for using the App appropriately on your device.

11. Your Choices

You can control the App's access and stored data by:

  • Choosing which folders to authorize
  • Removing previously authorized folders
  • Declining or revoking Full Disk Access in macOS settings
  • Changing or resetting app preferences
  • Deleting the App and its local data from your Mac

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page.
